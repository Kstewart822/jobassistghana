Technical Issues
How do I create an account on JobAssist Ghana?
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and fill in your basic personal information. After verification, you can complete your profile and start applying for jobs.
Is JobAssist Ghana free to use?
Yes, our basic services are completely free! You can create a profile, browse jobs, and apply for positions without any cost. We offer premium features like profile boosting for enhanced visibility, but these are optional.
How can I update my personal information?
Go to "My Profile" in your dashboard. You can edit most of your information there. Note that some sensitive details like Ghana Card number and digital address cannot be changed for security reasons after initial submission.
How do I track my job applications?
All your applications are tracked in the "Your Applications" section. You can see the status of each application (Pending, Shortlisted, Accepted, Rejected) and take appropriate actions.
Can I withdraw a job application?
Yes, you can withdraw applications that are still under review. Go to "Your Applications", find the application you want to withdraw, and click the "Withdraw" button. This action cannot be undone.
What are the benefits of boosting my profile?
Profile boosting enhances your visibility further, putting you at the top of search results and giving you priority access to new job postings. Boosted profiles get 3x more views from employers.
How long do premium features last?
Profile boosting packages vary: Basic (7 days), Professional (30 days), and Enterprise (90 days). You can renew anytime.
The website is loading slowly. What should I do?
Try refreshing the page, clearing your browser cache, or using a different browser. If the problem persists, contact our technical support team with details about your device and browser.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. The link will expire in 1 hour for security reasons.
