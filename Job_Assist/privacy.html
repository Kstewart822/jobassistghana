Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information

Last Updated: January 2026

Data Protection Compliance

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you access or use our job platform ("the Platform"). We are committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of all users, including job seekers, employers, and visitors.

By using the Platform, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy in accordance with the Ghana Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843).

1. Introduction

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

This policy applies to:

  • Job seekers
  • Employers and recruiters
  • Visitors to our website
  • Users of any related services, tools, or applications

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information Provided by Users

When you create an account, apply for a job, or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location or address
  • CV/resume content
  • Date of birth (optional)
  • Employment history
  • Skills, qualifications, and certifications
  • Profile picture (optional)

2.2 Employer and Company Information

For employers, we may collect:

  • Company name
  • Business address
  • Contact person's name
  • Contact email and phone number
  • Job descriptions and postings

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you access the Platform, we automatically collect:

Information Type Examples
Device Information Desktop, mobile, browser type
Technical Information IP address, operating system
Usage Data Pages viewed and time spent on the Platform
Activity Logs Login times and activity logs
Cookies and Tracking Cookies and tracking data

2.4 Payment Information

For users who choose premium services:

  • We collect payment confirmations and transaction IDs
  • All payments are processed by secure third-party providers
  • We do not store or process credit/debit card details

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your user account
  • Process job applications
  • Connect job seekers with employers
  • Provide employers with access to candidate profiles
  • Enable premium application features
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Send notifications and job alerts
  • Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the Ghana Data Protection Act, we process personal data based on:

Legal Basis Description
Your Consent When you explicitly agree to data processing
Contract To fulfill our agreement with you (e.g., job application or employer service)
Legitimate Interests Our legitimate business interests that don't override your rights
Legal Obligations To comply with applicable laws and regulations

5. Sharing of Information

5.1 With Employers

When you apply for a job or create a public profile, your information may be shared with employers, including:

  • Name
  • Contact details
  • Resume/CV
  • Skills and experience

5.2 With Service Providers

We may share limited data with trusted third parties such as:

  • Payment processors
  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Analytics partners

These partners are obligated to protect your information and may not use it for any other purpose.

5.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your data:

  • To comply with court orders, laws, or regulations
  • To prevent illegal activity, fraud, or security threats
  • When required by law enforcement or government agencies

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Enhance security

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect your experience on the Platform.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to:

Purpose Retention Period
Provide our services While account is active + 2 years
Comply with legal obligations As required by law (typically 7 years)
Resolve disputes Until dispute resolution + 2 years
Enforce agreements Duration of agreement + 5 years

You may request deletion of your account and data at any time (see Section 9).

8. Data Protection & Security

We implement strong technical and organizational measures to protect data, including:

  • Encryption
  • Secure servers and firewalls
  • Access controls
  • Regular security audits

Important: No online platform is 100% secure. Users are encouraged to keep their passwords confidential and use strong, unique passwords.

9. Your Rights (Under Act 843)

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission

To exercise your rights, contact us at the details provided in Section 14.

10. International Data Transfers

Some data may be stored or processed in secure servers outside Ghana.

Where this occurs, we ensure your information is protected in accordance with Act 843 and international data protection standards.

11. Children's Privacy

The Platform is not intended for individuals under the age of 18.

If we discover that a minor has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

12. Third-Party Links

Our Platform may contain links to third-party websites.

Important: We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites, and users are encouraged to review their privacy policies.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Platform after changes means you accept the updated policy.
