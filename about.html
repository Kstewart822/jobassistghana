About Job Assist Ghana

We connect skilled job seekers with credible employers across Ghana—helping candidates get discovered faster, and helping businesses hire with confidence.

Verified connections Built for mobile Hiring made simpler

Our Mission & Vision

We’re focused on practical outcomes: helping candidates present themselves professionally and helping employers find the right fit.

Our Mission

To reduce the employment gap in Ghana by connecting verified job seekers with legitimate employers through a trusted, mobile-friendly platform that improves hiring speed and career outcomes.

Our Vision

To become Ghana’s most trusted platform where candidates gain visibility and employers discover dependable talent— supporting long-term growth for people and businesses.

Our Values

These principles guide how we build the platform, verify users, and improve results for both candidates and employers.

Trust & Verification

We prioritize credible profiles and genuine opportunities to protect both candidates and employers.

Simplicity

We keep the experience fast and easy—especially on mobile—so users can act quickly.

Fair Access

We help talented people get discovered, regardless of background, location, or school.

Outcome Driven

Everything we build is designed to increase interviews, hiring speed, and successful matches.
