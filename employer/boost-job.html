Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the boost last?
Standard Boost lasts for 30 days and Premium Boost lasts for 60 days. During this time, your job listing will maintain its featured position in search results.
Can I boost multiple job listings?
Yes, you can boost multiple job listings. Each listing requires its own boost purchase. Many employers boost their most urgent positions to maximize visibility.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the boost service. Contact our support team for assistance with refund requests.
How soon will I see results?
Your job listing will be boosted immediately after payment. Most employers see increased candidate applications within 24-48 hours as job seekers view featured listings first.
Can I pause or cancel a boost?
Boosts cannot be paused, but you can choose not to renew when your boost period ends. For cancellations during the active period, contact our support team.
