Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the boost last?
Professional Boost lasts for 30 days and Enterprise Boost lasts for 90 days. During this time, your application will maintain its priority position in the applicant list.
Can I boost multiple applications?
Yes, you can boost multiple job applications. Each application requires its own boost purchase. Many users boost their most important applications to maximize their chances.
Is there a money-back guarantee?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the boost service. Contact our support team for assistance with refund requests.
How soon will I see results?
Your application will be boosted immediately after payment. Most users see increased employer interest within 24-48 hours as hiring managers review the prioritized applicant list.
